Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help both the local businesses and the residents that they serve. This site will give local businesses a platform to advertise their services. This will provide these businesses access to the services being provided in the community and provide the opportunity to form connections with other business owners. The hope is that these individual business owners really take advantage of being a part of this community and all it has to offer--looking inward to the community before outsourcing to other areas, thus helping to strengthen the entire community. This site will also be very helpful to anyone looking to see what businesses and services are available in the Wickham Park Area. It's not uncommon for residents to be unaware of all of the services offered right at their fingertips, so this website will be very useful.
