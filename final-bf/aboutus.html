Remembering Our Roots

Bountiful Foods has its roots deep in the California soil. It was on a farm where John and Elizabeth Rivera fell in love with the fruits and vegetables that they tended to in their backyard in Carlsbad, CA. They focused on the quality of their produce at every step, from making sure that the soil was fertile and clear of pesticides, to the quality of the all-natural insect repellent they would use. They found there way to a strong community of other like-minded growers who they would frequent at the local farmer's markets. One day, not any day in particular, but as Elizabeth was walking through the farmer's market, she got the idea of what would be the beginnings of Bountiful Foods. She envisioned a consistent market that would feature the amazing produce of the local farmers who were now friends. It was about community and love and it still is to this day. If you haven't already, come, visit, see the difference for yourself here at Bountiful Foods.
