Discover What Makes Wickham Park So Wonderful!

Wickham Park Origins

Wickham Park was originally founded by Peter Wickham and his family in 1640. Peter was a wealthy adventurer who wanted to try his luck in the "New World". After finding his way to what would be called Wickham Park, decided to make it his home after discovering that the land provided everything that they needed to thrive. Peter Wickham opened up the first General Store, the first Post Office, and the first School. He helped others in the community to set up businesses that were needed by donating to them. These and other early businesses did so well that they are still being run today! Before his passing, Peter was quoted with one final thought on the town: "While Wickham Park is beautiful, the true treasure is found in its people. We support eachother here and have built eachother up to what it is now. We are our greatest asset!"

Community that Supports Local Businesses

Wickham Park is home to a very diverse community that is known for supporting the local businesses. Business owners that have embraced the members of the community and have made an effort to get to know and support them have been the ones to thrive! There is truly no other community like this one!

Tech Hub

Tech is the number one industry here, and it's growing! Many people from New York City found their way to Wickham Park during the beginnings of COVID. The beautiful atmosphere and the warm community has really made Wickham Park the desired area for those who work-from-home.

Business Projections

Even with the ebbs and flows of the economy, Wickham Park businesses have remained strong. All businesses reported a net gain throughout COVID and continue to do so. It is projected that with so many discovering the beauty of Wickham Park that these net profits will only continue to increase. The population has increased also, with many new people moving into Wickham Park weekly. The housing market is doing extremely well. Property values have increased significantly with the increase in demand. All of these things considered, Wickham Park is looking to be one of the most successful communities in the East Coast for years to come!

Town Hall Meetings

The Wickham Park Town Hall hosts monthly meetings! Want to get to know the community? This is where you need to be! Important decisions are made here, business deals are made here, but most importantly, friendships are made here. New chamber members will also be announced here. The Town Hall Meeting is always on the 1st Thursday of the month at 7pm. All are welcome!
