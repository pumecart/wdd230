White Space & Clean Design

Google

www.google.com

The page is clean and simple. The white space draws your attention to the Google logo and the seach bar.

Alignment

Realtor

www.realtor.com

The top of the page is aligned to the center and as you scroll down the page, the homes are aligned and centered as well in each row.

Contrast

Amazon

www.amazon.com

I like the use of contrast in the amazon page. Both the light and dark color contrasts bring in user attention and I feel like it maintains user interest by making the items on the screen more visually exciting. I never get bored scrolling through everything!
